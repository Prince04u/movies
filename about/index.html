About MaleHotMovies

MaleHotMovies is a premium entertainment magazine covering global cinema, streaming, and the business of film. Our editors watch every movie they review, write every word, and answer every reader email.

What we cover

We publish daily across six core beats: theatrical reviews, streaming guides, casting and production news, festival coverage, box office analysis, and long-form profiles. We're equally interested in a $200M franchise opener and an unheralded indie playing at TIFF.

How we rate

Every review uses a 10-point scale weighing direction, screenplay, performance, craft, and rewatch value. We never accept payment to influence reviews, and we disclose every press screening, junket, and travel arrangement in the review itself.

Our editorial standards

We correct errors prominently and quickly. We do not publish unsourced rumors. We separate news reporting from criticism and label opinion as such. Every contributor's byline links to a profile listing their full work.

Questions about a review, request a correction, or want to pitch a story? Email the editor at our contact page.
