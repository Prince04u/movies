Upcoming DC Movies: Full Calendar & Slate

By MaleHotMovies Editorial· January 15, 2026· 8 min read

DC enters its rebuilt era with a clearer creative compass and the first slate that genuinely looks like a coherent plan.

The Year in Context

To understand this year you have to look at the last three. Production pipelines reshaped after the strikes, streamers consolidated around fewer-but-larger originals, and audience behavior continued to bifurcate between event theatrical and curated streaming.

The films and trends that mattered most were not always the ones with the biggest marketing campaigns. Some of the year’s defining moments came from films released without fanfare on a Tuesday in February.
