Upcoming Hollywood Movies 2026: Full Release Calendar

By MaleHotMovies Editorial· January 15, 2026· 8 min read

Hollywood’s 2026 slate is unusually balanced: fewer mega-franchise tentpoles, more original IP, and a wave of mid-budget genre films designed to play globally. Here is the calendar we are watching.

Spring Releases

Spring brings the year’s first prestige genre swings, including Neon Republic and Echo Protocol.

Summer Tentpoles

Summer is anchored by the long-awaited sequel to one of 2023’s biggest hits and an original sci-fi from a major auteur.

Fall & Awards Season

By October the calendar shifts toward awards contenders — character pieces, period dramas, and the year’s first serious Best Picture conversations.

Streaming Hybrids

Several mid-budget titles will use a hybrid release: brief theatrical, fast PVOD, then OTT. See our OTT releases piece for streaming-first watchlists.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are these dates final?

Studios shift release dates often. We update this article whenever a major film moves.

Each linked review embeds the trailer where available, or links to the official studio channel.
