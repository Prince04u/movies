Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026.

MaleHotMovies respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect when you visit our website, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

Information we collect

We collect minimal personal information: an email address only if you subscribe to our newsletter, and standard server logs (IP address, browser, referring page). We use first-party analytics to understand which articles readers find valuable.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for site functionality and optional cookies for analytics. You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser without losing access to the site.

Third-party services

Embedded trailers, social posts, and advertising may set their own cookies governed by their respective privacy policies.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you by emailing privacy@malehotmovies.com. We respond within 30 days.

Changes

We update this policy when our practices change. Material changes are announced on our homepage for at least 14 days.
